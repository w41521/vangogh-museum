About Van Gogh

Let us tell you the story about Van Gogh!

Vincent Willem van Gogh(30 March 1853 ~ 29 July 1890) was a Dutch Post-Impressionist painter. They include landscapes, still lifes, portraits, and self-portraits, and are characterised by bold coloursand dramatic, and expressive brushwork. Not commercially successful, he struggled with severe depression and poverty, eventually leading to his suicide at age thirty-seven. Read more about Van Gogh.

The achievement in his life.

In a decade, he created about 2,100 artworks, including around 860 oil paintings, most of which date from the last two years of his life.
